Assignment 2: DUE 9/25

  • Watch the following videos, come prepared to discuss.
    • F.A.T. Lab, Hacking Art and Culture
    • Eyeo Festival Videos
    • Bruce Sterling, CLOUDS Interview
  • Create a composition further exploring the themes you showed in class that reacts to the mouse by transforming between two different states or perspectives, both visually and conceptually. That is, your viewer should feel like they are seeing things one way, then upon moving the mouse, arrive at or be confronted with an unexpectedly different perspective. Email the link to a page running your code to Evelyn or Lauren by Wednesday at 11am. Some examples to ponder:

    • The Yes Men
